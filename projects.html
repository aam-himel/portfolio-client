Earthquake prediction using deep learning

Data science • 2021 • min read

I created an algorithm based on deep learning that can discern foreshock, mainshock or aftershock in real-time. Until now, it is impossible to identify the type of earthquake in real-time. Identifying foreshocks as and when they occur provides valuable insight to predict imminent earthquakes. After training, I successfully classified them into their respective types with high accuracy (>99%). The project is one of the first applications of deep learning in the field of seismology, and I am thrilled and excited about it.
